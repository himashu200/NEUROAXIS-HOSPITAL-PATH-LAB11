About NEUROAXIS HOSPITAL & PATH LAB

NEUROAXIS HOSPITAL & PATH LAB is a state-of-the-art healthcare facility located in the heart of Surat, Gujarat. We integrate advanced neurological treatments with high-precision pathology services under one roof. Our mission is to deliver accurate diagnostics, compassionate patient care, and accessible medical excellence.

Our dedicated team of neurologists, radiologists, and pathologists work in synergy to provide comprehensive care for brain, spine, and nerve disorders. Simultaneously, our NABL-aligned pathology lab offers a wide spectrum of tests — from routine checkups to specialized genetic and autoimmune panels.

At NeuroAxis, we believe in ethical practices, transparency, and patient-first approach. With cutting-edge equipment and a patient-centric environment, we are redefining neurology and diagnostic services in the region.

Our core values: Integrity, Innovation, Empathy, and Accuracy.
